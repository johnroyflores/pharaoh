Founded in 2015, our goal was to create the highest quality concentrates. We have assembled a team of industry experts to bring the people of Colorado a trustworthy and competent company. These are the reasons our company decided to move forward with CO2 extraction. Searching through the vast amounts of concentrates on the market we noticed the sheer amount of concentrates being produced without any oversight or testing. This never sat well with us as consumers. We wanted to be able to provide people with a true healthy alternative to the propane and butane concentrates that have peppered the landscape. The other issue we saw with the cannabis industry and CO2 producers was the mixing of other chemicals with their product to make them vapeable. Using these untested chemicals made no sense to us, the answer wasn't to muddy your product but instead clean it up. That approach has become standard for us, using our proprietary quadruple refining technique we are able to provide our customers with the highest quality concentrate on the market. We always tell people to stay informed and Just Vape.
