Where can I find Pyramid Pens?

We are currently only located in Colorado but are looking at expanding into other legal states. If you can't find us in your local dispensary let them know you would like us on your shelves!

Can a Cartridge be shipped to me?

Unfortunately it is illegal to transport marijuana across state lines. Work on legilization in your home state so companies like us can give you the experiences you deserve.

How should I store my Pen?

You should always store your pen at room temperature. Colorado summers are hot, your car is no place for pets or your Pyramid Pens!

Does my cartridge come with a battery?

Pyramid Pens batteries are unfortunately sold separately. You can purchase them at any location where our Cartridges are sold. Our 100mg disposable pen is perfect for those times you forgot your battery!

Are Pyramid Pens reusable?

Our cartridges are made for disposal after each use. We plan on selling a Pyramid Pens cartridge and refill kit in the near future.

How many uses will I get out of my Cartridge?

The answer varies from person to person and depends on the size of your cartridge. You can anticipate 100-250 puffs per cartridge.

How do I know my battery is fully charged?

When you plug in your Pyramid Pens battery, the USB charging toggle will be lit red. When it changes to green your battery is fully charged and ready for use.
